# agents.md

## Goal

Build a minimal, elegant portfolio website for an artist that:

* clearly communicates who the artist is
* showcases artwork
* explains what commissions/services are available
* makes contacting the artist extremely easy

The website should prioritize **visual storytelling and simplicity**.

---

## Core Principles

1. **Art first**
   Artwork must dominate the interface.
   Avoid heavy UI or distracting elements.

2. **Fast understanding**
   A visitor must understand in **5 seconds**:

* what kind of artist this is
* what they create
* how to commission work

3. **Minimal navigation**
   Prefer a **single-page structure** with anchor navigation.

4. **Large visuals**
   Artwork should be displayed in:

* full-width hero images
* clean grid galleries
* large previews

5. **Commission clarity**
   Visitors must quickly understand:

* what they can order
* examples
* rough price ranges
* process

6. **Low-friction contact**
   Contact must be extremely simple:

* email
* Telegram / Instagram / social
* simple commission form

---

## Recommended Sections

1. Hero
2. About the Artist
3. Gallery / Selected Works
4. Commission Types
5. Process
6. Testimonials (optional)
7. Contact

---

## Design Style

Preferred style:

* minimal
* gallery-like
* neutral background
* large typography
* focus on artwork

Color palette:

* neutral backgrounds
* soft accent color

---

## UX Rules

* Max 2 clicks to contact the artist
* Images open in lightbox
* Mobile-first layout
* Fast loading images
* Simple scroll flow

---

## Technical Preferences

Framework: any lightweight stack

Recommended:

* Next.js
* Astro
* simple static site

Image optimization required.

---

## Success Criteria

A visitor should be able to:

1. Understand the artist style in 5 seconds
2. Browse artwork easily
3. Understand commission possibilities
4. Contact the artist in under 30 seconds
