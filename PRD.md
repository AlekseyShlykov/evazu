# Product Requirements Document

Artist Portfolio Website

## Product Vision

Create a simple portfolio website that helps an artist:

* present their identity and artistic style
* showcase their best works
* attract commission clients
* simplify communication with potential customers

The site acts as both **portfolio + commission landing page**.

---

## Target Audience

Primary users:

1. People looking to commission artwork
2. Potential collaborators
3. Art fans discovering the artist

---

## Key User Goals

Visitors should quickly be able to:

* understand what the artist creates
* see examples of work
* learn what they can order
* contact the artist easily

---

## Core Pages / Sections

### 1. Hero Section

Purpose: immediate impression.

Content:

* artist name
* short tagline
* strong artwork preview
* button: "View Gallery"
* button: "Commission Artwork"

---

### 2. About the Artist

Short story about the artist.

Content:

* who they are
* artistic style
* inspirations
* experience

Optional:

* portrait photo

---

### 3. Gallery

Grid of artworks.

Features:

* categories (optional)
* lightbox preview
* high resolution images

Goal: show strongest work first.

---

### 4. Commissions

Explain what clients can order.

Examples:

* character illustrations
* book covers
* game art
* concept art
* portraits

Each service may include:

* example images
* description
* starting price

---

### 5. Commission Process

Explain how ordering works.

Example steps:

1. Contact artist
2. Discuss idea
3. Sketch approval
4. Final artwork
5. Delivery

Goal: reduce uncertainty for clients.

---

### 6. Testimonials (optional)

Quotes from previous clients.

Purpose:

* build trust

---

### 7. Contact

Multiple ways to contact:

* Email
* Telegram / Instagram
* Commission form

Form fields:

* name
* email
* project description
* reference images (optional)

---

## Non-Functional Requirements

* Mobile friendly
* Fast loading images
* SEO basics
* Simple CMS or easy image update

---

## Success Metrics

The website is successful if visitors:

* spend time exploring the gallery
* understand commission options
* contact the artist easily
